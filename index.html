TRINITAN GROUP

Since founded in 1970, Trinitan Group have focused on energy storage solutions that promote green energy as one of the company’s core business initiatives.

At Trinitan we care about our planet, our friends, our families and our communities. We strive to create a balance between growing and expanding our operations, achieving maximum profitability for our shareholders and being a socially responsible organization with values based on integrity, ambition and intelligence.
